Illuminate Your Memory

Memories in Light

Lithophanes are a magical art form where images emerge when light passes through translucent porcelain. At Porcelain Prints, we transform your cherished memories into illuminated masterpieces.

Each piece is custom-crafted to capture every detail, creating a stunning visual experience that changes with the light—from subtle shadows in daylight to glowing brilliance when lit from behind.

Whether displayed with nightlights or candlelight, our lithophanes bring warmth and wonder to any space.

Our Creations

Custom Lithophanes

Transform your favorite photographs into stunning three-dimensional lithophane art. We meticulously convert your images into depth variations that create breathtaking detail when illuminated.

Nightlight Displays

Our lithophane nightlights combine artistry with functionality. Perfect for bedrooms, nurseries, or hallways, they cast a gentle, personalized glow throughout the night.

Candle Lithophanes

Experience the romance of candlelit porcelain. Our candle-compatible designs create an intimate ambiance, with your memories dancing in flickering light.
